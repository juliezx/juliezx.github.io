Block Tower 2018 | SketchUp

For this project, I was assigned to create a tower on SketchUp using exactly 50 cubes, and making it at least 5 “stories” tall. I was listening to Dan Brown’s “Inferno” audiobook when I designed it, so it ended up looking like it was inspired by the book. The book frequently refers to Dante’s “Inferno,” which talks about 7 layers of hell that shapes like a upsidedown circular cone. My design consists of 6 layers and each layer lessens in size. Each side looks similar but slightly different --- like order and chaos.
